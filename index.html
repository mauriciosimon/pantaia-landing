Workflow modules

Jobs Board

Live budget vs. committed vs. actual with margin red-flagging and PM-scoped visibility.

Cost Sheets

First upload is the baseline; every re-upload is auto-flagged as a variation with delta-vs-baseline and delta-vs-previous, parsed by a Claude agent.

Call-Offs

PMs create material, labour, and plant requests from the field. Procurement reviews, fills the supplier and amount, and submits.

Purchase Orders

Read-only log. Generated server-side on call-off submission — sequential PO numbers, PDFs to object storage, supplier confirmation and split-delivery tracking.

Invoices

Manual upload, manual PO selection, 5% tolerance auto-match. Materials route to procurement; labour and T&J route to the linked PM. Disputed invoices loop back to the supplier.

Notifications

In-app, role-routed: call-off pending, invoice pending approval, variation applied, margin below threshold.
