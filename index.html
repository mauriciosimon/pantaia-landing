Featured Project CEO & System Designer

Pakoa

Gamified MLM Platform that Scaled a Business 4x

The Context

As CEO of OneContact Mexico, a telecom contact center, I faced a crisis: high turnover, flat growth, and a young workforce (18-24) that wasn't engaged. Traditional management wasn't working.

What I Built

I designed and led the development of Pakoa, a gamified multi-level marketing platform that transformed how we managed sales teams. The system gave employees visibility into their progress, clear advancement paths, and motivation structures tailored to their demographic.

Key Features

  • Honeycomb visualization — each salesperson's community tree (up to 259 slots)
  • Real-time sales dashboard — funnel tracking (Prospecto → Cotizado → Agendado → Instalado)
  • "Llave del Reino" — weekly threshold system ($15,000 in 30-day sales) unlocking commission chains
  • Commission engine — 8% from direct reports, 12% from second level, 20% from third level
  • AI Assistant — Claude API providing personalized coaching and recommendations
