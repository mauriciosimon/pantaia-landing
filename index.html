The Challenge

OneContact Colombia, a leading telecommunications contact center, struggled with fragmented data across their Odoo ERP system. Their managers spent hours manually compiling reports, lacking real-time visibility into agent performance and call metrics.

The Solution

Our team built an end-to-end integration connecting Odoo ERP to Power BI, featuring:

  • Real-time data sync between Odoo and Power BI
  • AI-generated insights delivered in Spanish
  • Automated anomaly detection for call patterns
  • Mobile-friendly dashboards for managers on the go
